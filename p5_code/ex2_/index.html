Sketch X

(Artist Sketch) The goal of this assignment was to learn how to work with images, and some sort of grid. The first image that pops into my mind everyday is MONEY! So, I made a grid of alternating bills, and continued from there.
You can press the mouse to flip the image into something else, and you can press spacebar for airhorns!


The logic behind the alternating bills surprisingly took more effor tthan I thought. In addition, with the way things are layered on the screen, it was a bit tough to work with rendering the images. The Mr. Krabs gif is being updated at a different speed than the background, so when a change happens to the image such as a tint or size change, it changes first, but I decided to leave that in. I'm happy that gifs are not hard to implement in p5.
