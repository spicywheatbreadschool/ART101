Proj3

A clock made just so you can follow my personal schedule, which I'm sure is very useful! Here, I used date functions to make a functioning clock in the layout of a music player. We also learned a bit about mapping, which I found extremely useful in that I didn't have to do all the probably simple but frustrating math of converting one range to another. Fianlly, I wrapped it all up with classes, and put everything inside tidy functions to make it probably my cleanest project.The types of songs displayed are based on the current time of day, and there are captions that reflect the typical action of myself at that time of day and day of the week.
