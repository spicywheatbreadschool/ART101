Project 3

(La di da)
This project was actually one of the more fun, personal, and simple projects! Here, we tried to make a clock to represent time in a non traditional manner, and in a way that showcases our personal lives. My choice was to make a clock based on the music I listen to in my life. You listen to different music when you do different things, when you want to experience a different mood, and listen to different music at different times of the day, which I thought fit naturally with the idea of a clock. In addition, I thought there were clear ways I could indicate the flow of time in a music based presentation, with the volume bar representing the seconds, the song progression representing minutes, and the track number being the hour. Even while working, I found that I was able to tell what time it was by looking at my own clock!
